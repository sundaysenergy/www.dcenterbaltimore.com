Learning by Design

35
10.04.2011

How can design bring change to challenging environments? How can it foster learning?

Lee Hyden, Director of Interiors at Ayers Saint Gross, and graphic designer Jillian Erhardt, take us on a virtual tour of a probono project in the new Baltimore Design School.

Remy Peritz, former Content Strategist for the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation and former Post-Production Manager of TED's TEDx program, shows us how to wrangle a message into a design for a winning presentation.

Danah Abdulla, Founder, Creative Director and Editor of Kalimat Magazine talks about design and the Arab region and will make you rethink the way you know Arabs.

Lee Hyden
Jillian Erhardt
Remy Peritz
Danah Abdulla
