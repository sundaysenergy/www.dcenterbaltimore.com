Get Involved

There are several ways to get involved with D center:

1. Volunteer
D center is always looking for volunteer help with exhibitions, events, design conversations, programming ideas, and outreach. Send us a note at [email protected] for more information about current opportunities.

2. Run your own Design Conversation
Have an idea for a Design Conversation that you would like to organize? Contact [email protected] with your suggested topic and potential speakers.

3. Borrow our space
Interested in renting our space on North Avenue for an event or exhibition? Send us a proposal at [email protected]; please include why you'd like to hold your event at D center and whether you'd like to simply rent the space or partner with us on a larger project.

4. Donate
As a not-for-profit organization, D center depends on the support of our community. Any amount will help us to continue to develop our programming and general operations. Thank you in advance for your contribution!
